COFFEE WALNUT MUG CAKE

INGREDIENTS:-
Flour- 3tbsp
Baking powder - 1/8 tsp
Baking soda - a pinch
Condensed milk - 3 tbsp
Melted butter- 2 tbsp
Milk ( + 2tsp coffee powder) - 4 tbsp
Walnuts - a fistful

PROCEDURE:-
- Stir together condensed milk, melted butter and coffee with milk in a microwaveable mug.
- Add flour, baking soda and baking powder and mix well
- Mix the walnuts and microwave at 850 watts for about 2 minutes.
- Serve warm
